Hi, I'm Lincoln — I've lived the community college path.

Questions

Good things to know before we start.

Who is this for?

Community college students at any stage — first-years just getting oriented, students about to transfer, and anyone in between. If you want an internship and aren't sure how to get there, you're in the right place.

What does the free intro call cover?

It's a relaxed 20 minutes to hear your goals, your field, and where you feel stuck. By the end you'll have a clear sense of your next steps — whether or not you decide to keep working together.

Do I need to have a field or major figured out?

No. Plenty of students come in unsure of their direction. Figuring out where you fit and which roles to target is part of the work we can do together.

How do sessions happen?

Over video, so you can join from anywhere — no travel required. We'll set times that fit around your classes and work schedule.

Will this help if I want to transfer to a four-year school?

Yes. A strong internship and a sharp resume make you a far more competitive transfer applicant, and we can build your search with that goal in mind.

How do I get started?

Book a free intro call on the calendar above, or send a request through the form. Either way, I'll be in touch and we'll take it from there.

The fine print

How this works & your privacy.

Who you're working with

The Fetcher is operated by Lincoln Kwok as an independent, one-person coaching service. You're working directly with me — not an agency or a team.

What this service is

The Fetcher provides coaching, guidance, and feedback to help you search for and apply to internships. I'll do everything I can to set you up for success, but I can't guarantee any specific internship, offer, admission, or outcome — those depend on many factors outside our sessions.

Payment & scheduling

The intro call is free. Paid sessions are $20 for a single 30-minute call, or $55 for a package of three. Payment is arranged directly after your free intro call. If you need to reschedule, just let me know ahead of time and we'll find a new slot.

Your privacy

When you book a call or send a request, you share details like your name, email, and what you're working on. I use that information only to coach you and stay in touch about our sessions. I don't sell it, and I don't share it with anyone else. Booking is handled through Google Calendar, and messages you send through the form go to my email — both are subject to those providers' own privacy policies.

Want your information removed? Email me at lincolnkwok15@gmail.com and I'll delete it.
